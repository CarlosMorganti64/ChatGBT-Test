Types of Cheese

Cheddar

Cheddar cheese is a popular hard cheese that originated in England. It ranges from mild to sharp in flavor.

Brie

Brie is a soft cheese from France known for its creamy interior and edible rind.

Gorgonzola

Gorgonzola is a blue cheese that adds a strong flavor to salads and pastas.
